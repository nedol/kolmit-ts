Kolmit Presentation

1. Introduction

Kolmit is an innovative app that helps people practice speaking foreign languages.

Learning a language is not just about grammar and vocabulary but also about the ability to communicate freely. However, many people struggle with the lack of live conversation practice. Kolmit solves this problem, making the language learning process more interactive and effective.

2. Key Features

  • Multilingual support – Users can select from multiple languages for learning.
  • AI-generated content – Lessons, exercises, quizzes, and dialogues are automatically created.
  • Exercise types:
    • Vocabulary expansion – flashcards and word association exercises.
    • Listening and pronunciation improvement – audio-based tasks and speech recognition.
    • Sentence building – exercises to complete sentences correctly.
  • Social engagement – Interactive community-based learning and real-time feedback.
  • Online mode – The app operates in real-time, allowing users to learn together online.

3. Classroom Use Case

Kolmit can replace traditional paper-based materials in classrooms.

Teachers can create digital exercises for students, track progress, and enhance learning efficiency.

4. Conclusion

Kolmit is more than just an app – it is a tool that makes language learning engaging and effective.
